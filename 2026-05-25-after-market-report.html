🇺🇸 Memorial Day — Holiday Note

Memorial Day (the last Monday of May) is a U.S. federal holiday honoring military personnel who have died in service to the country. U.S. financial markets — NYSE, NASDAQ, and the bond market — are closed on this day. Trading resumes at the standard open, 9:30 AM ET on Tuesday, May 26, 2026.
